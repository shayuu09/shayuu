melona


fruity fresh joy on a stick!

This is an unofficial rebrand.
Melona is one of, if not the most popular product of the Korean snack company Binggrae. Released in 1992, it's actually gone through a rebrand once before. While the current branding is pretty solid, I wanted to see if I could push it further.

Rebrand


Development

Rebranding Melona was no small feat. Its website literally boasts "Why change perfection?", and the brand has already undergone a change in the past. So, instead of overhauling the entire look, I decided to find holes in the seemingly-perfect brand. These would become the foundation of my rebrand.
