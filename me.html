about me


introduction

I'm shayuu (aka Alex), a graphic and fashion designer from Canada. I'm currently situated in Waterloo for university, but I sometimes venture out to go to Toronto.

inspirations

Sharks are my favourite animal ever, which is why my artist name is "shark" in Mandarin!
Aside from sharks, my current inspiration is MelonLand! The artist behind it, Daniel Murray, holds such an interesting art form.
In fashion design, I strive to keep sustainability in mind. I do this via upcycling most if not all materials used.
