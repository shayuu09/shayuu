God Gave Him a Wolf's Heart

Conception

God Gave Him A Wolf's Heart by sun is poison is probably one of (if not) my favourite albums in the world, but there are no physical copies of it in existence. I designed this version to imagine what a CD case for the album would look like. This is not an official design.

Development

One issue I ran into was the actual visuals on the case: did I want to keep the original cover image or did I want to make my own? I decided to "hide" the original image in several parts of the design, see if you can spot it!
I felt the imagery should be literal, so I added an anatomical heart and a wolf into the design. I also wanted an inversion effect, sort of like an x-ray. This way it could be like an "x-ray" into "his" wolf heart.

final touches

To add that final flair, I added in the lyrics of each song onto each portion of the design. I used a font called "Redacted Script" to achieve the effect of small cursive.
Overall, I'm pretty proud!
